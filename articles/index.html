What is Investing about?

Investing is expanding money with the expectation of profit by using it to develop a company. When we teach students about teaching, we go over all the steps required to research and identify great companies, and the types of investing strategies. In this process, students get an understanding of the corporate world: not only do they focus on the financial aspects of companies, they also understand marketing, technology, Customer Relations Management, E-commerce, etc. as they research the wide range of business models of companies.

What is Personal Finance about?

Personal Finance is about learning all the financial concepts that will help students sustain themselves financially in the future and achieve financial freedom. Most students are unaware of personal finance topics like asset classes, checking accounts vs. savings accounts, and how credit and debit works. We go over all of these concepts to help students in their future!

What is Economics about?

Economics is the study of allocation of resources and its consumption. Gunn High School offers Economics courses, so we do not go into much depth in this topic. We go over basic concepts that will help foster an understanding of personal finance and investing like interest rates, what currency is, inflation, and the Federal Reserve.

What Programs Do We Have?

We offer students with the opportunity to get involved in paper trading competitions which allows them to practice their concepts without the risk of loosing money. However, we do encourage students to invest real money. Additionally, we will host interactive competitions like stock pitch competitions, investment strategy pitch competitions, and case competitions to get a better understanding of the business world as a whole.
